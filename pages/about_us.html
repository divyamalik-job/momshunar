Google, What does "Mom's Hunar" means?

Google replied: Hunar is a Hindi word which means talent so it means Mom's talent.

What do you do in your leisure time?

Well, I guess, hmm watching TV, scrolling Instagram stories or sleeping or many more things that might not add that much value to your life and society.

You know, when my talented mom (Sarita Malik) and I (Divya Malik), get some time, we sit together and brainstorm what we can craft BEST out of WASTE products available in our home such as old magazines, threads of paper bags, ice-cream sticks, left-over clothes and many more. My mom generally says 'A Penny Saved is a Penny Earned' and this phrase stuck in my mind since my childhood and I started converting discarded and scrap of household items into usable items. My mom keeps giving me suggestions-do this do that to utilise this waste. Besides that, my mom and I both love visiting handicraft exhibitions in order to appreciate arts of artisans.

Our mission is to seek more ideas how we can make use of waste, reuse products and upcycle left-over items as it will not help us to save money but also add value to society or environment by reducing some waste.
